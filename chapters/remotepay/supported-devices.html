Remote Pay works with two Token device families. Onboarding is the same for both (see How it works), and card payments work identically on both: same request, same callback result. The one difference is fiscal commands, which are available on x30 only.

x30

  • Card payments and fiscal commands.
  • Results are delivered to your own callback URL.

p2se

  • Card payments.
  • Results are delivered to your own callback URL, the same way as x30.

At a glance:

Capability x30 p2se
Card payments Yes Yes
Fiscal commands Yes No
Result delivery Your callback URL Your callback URL
Per-device callback URL Yes Yes

In short: payments are identical across x30 and p2se; fiscal commands are x30 only. The Payment command section covers both families; Fiscal commands applies to x30.
