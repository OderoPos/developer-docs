OPit Serial is a background Android service that enables ECR (Electronic Cash Register) devices to communicate with the OderoPOS terminal over a serial (RS-232) connection using the Hypercom protocol.

The service runs as a foreground service on the POS device and automatically starts at boot. It connects to the ECR via the Sunmi ECR Connection Bridge SDK, listens for incoming Hypercom messages and translates them into payment and bank operations executed through the OderoPOS payment gateway.

Supported operations:

  • Payment — initiates a card payment for the amount received from the ECR.
  • Void transaction — cancels a previously processed transaction by receipt number.
  • Settlement — triggers an end-of-day bank settlement.

Application behaviour:

  • The service starts automatically on device boot via a BootCompleteReceiver.
  • The MainActivity displays the current connection state and version info.
  • All serial communication is handled in the background — no user interaction is required during normal operation.
  • If the cable is disconnected, the service waits 5 seconds and then attempts to reconnect automatically.
